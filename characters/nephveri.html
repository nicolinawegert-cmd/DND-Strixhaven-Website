Backstory

Nephveri Baëla is a tiefling warlock who has made a pact with a mysterious entity known as The Whispering Shadow. She is known for her cunning and resourcefulness, often using her charm and wit to navigate social situations. Nephveri is fiercely loyal to her friends and will go to great lengths to protect them. Her dark past is shrouded in mystery, but she is determined to forge her own path and make a name for herself at Strixhaven.
