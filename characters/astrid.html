Backstory

Astrid Isulv a viking Moon Elf druid who was raised by the Isulv clan on Svelheim, a island outside the coast of the continent. She is the daughter of the clan chief and has always felt the weight of her responsibilities. She has a strong personallity and is known for her fierce loyalty to her friends and family. She loves wolfs and feel connected with them. She is determined to prove herself as a capable leader and make her clan proud.
